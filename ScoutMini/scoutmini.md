# Autonomous Mobile Robots - Scout Mini (ROS 2 Jazzy)

## Overview
This lecture introduces the Scout Mini, a 4-wheel skid-steering mobile robot. Students will learn how to configure the ROS 2 environment, understand the kinematic model of skid-steering, and execute basic teleoperation and velocity control in both simulation and the real world.
